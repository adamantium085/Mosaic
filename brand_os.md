##  The Loop Methodology

TESSERA replaces outdated agency pipelines with a continuously adaptive system called **The Loop**. It is the operating rhythm behind all brand operations at TESSERA—a five-phase, recursive model that enables momentum, clarity, and engineered brand performance.

### The Five Phases of the Loop
1. **Intelligence** – Insight synthesis and strategic clarity  
2. **Creative** – Verbal, visual, and tonal identity systems  
3. **Activation** – Channel-logic, asset deployment, and rollout  
4. **Optimisation** – Speed tuning, outcome alignment, QA  
5. **Insight** – Learning capture, data feedback, loopback triggers

Each phase feeds the next, but also loops forward and backward as needed. The system is modular and cyclical—built for refinement, not reinvention.
