# 🧬 TESSERA Explainer

**TESSERA** is a modular brand operations system designed to evolve alongside the businesses it powers.  
It structures all brand and marketing work into a system of **repeatable loops**, driven by strategy, creativity, and continuous improvement.

---

##  What Is TESSERA?

TESSERA is:
- A **Brand Operating System** — defining how brand assets, intelligence, and behaviours are structured.
- A **Modular Framework** — every phase (Strategy, Creative, Activation, Optimisation, Insight) can stand alone or loop continuously.
- A **Living Brand Model** — unlike static guidelines, TESSERA adapts to the brand’s growth, audience shifts, and market realities.

---

##  Loop Phases

| Phase        | Purpose                                 |
|--------------|------------------------------------------|
| Intelligence | Gather insight and inputs                |
| Creative     | Generate concepts, assets, messaging     |
| Activation   | Deploy work across platforms             |
| Optimisation | Improve based on performance             |
| Insight      | Learn and feed back into strategy        |

This loop is **recursive and agile** — enabling real-time brand evolution without chaos.

---

## 🔧 Why TESSERA?

- Traditional brand guidelines are **static and become outdated**.
- Businesses today operate in **multi-channel, high-change environments**.
- TESSERA lets brand teams move fast **without compromising clarity or consistency**.

---

##  How MosaicAI Uses It

MosaicAI references TESSERA to:
- Structure strategic and creative processes
- Categorise recommendations by phase
- Suggest next steps based on loop position
- Create brand deliverables modularly and adaptively

TESSERA is embedded into:
- `campaign.strategy.canvas`

- Diagnostic models
